About Me

As a Master of Engineering student in Computer Science at Cornell Tech, I am building upon my strong foundation in Mechanical Engineering to tackle complex, multidisciplinary challenges. My diverse background, having lived and studied in Kazakhstan, Singapore, and the US, has instilled in me a global perspective and the ability to collaborate effectively with people from all walks of life.

Throughout my professional journey, I have demonstrated a knack for adapting to new environments and delivering impactful results. From leading peace conferences in Singapore to being recognized as the most influential student at NYU, I bring a unique blend of technical prowess and leadership skills to every project I undertake.

My experience at Graphene Laboratories Inc., allowed me to apply my engineering expertise to develop innovative products, such as a graphene air purification filter to combat the spread of COVID-19. I have also experienced working in different settings, as my experience at Tambour in Israel have showed me different approaches to Mechanical Engineering

My ultimate career goal is to disrupt a pet market through introduction of new smart devices, leveraging my interdisciplinary skill set to drive meaningful change. I am currently working on my startup, Pibble, creating a new self-cleaning automatic wet food feeder for cats!

If you're looking for a dedicated, versatile professional who thrives at the intersection of mechanical engineering and computer science, I would love to connect and explore how we can collaborate to bring groundbreaking ideas to life.
