As a Master of Engineering student in Computer Science at Cornell Tech, I am building upon my strong foundation in Mechanical Engineering to tackle complex, multidisciplinary challenges. My diverse background, having lived and studied in Kazakhstan, Singapore, and the US, has instilled in me a global perspective and the ability to collaborate effectively with people from all walks of life.

Throughout my professional journey, I have demonstrated a knack for adapting to new environments and delivering impactful results. From leading peace conferences in Singapore to being recognized as the most influential student at NYU, I bring a unique blend of technical prowess and leadership skills to every project I undertake.
